Optional:

If you wanted to eat something else instead of cakes and snacks for some weird reason. You can bring the ingredient for the mash potatoes and we'll make them.

Ingredients:

  • Milk
  • Small Bacon Pieces
  • Sliced Green Onions
  • 4 Mashed Potatoes
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Steps for making mashed potatoes:

  1. Peal off the Potatoes
  2. Boil the Potatoes
    • This part can take like an hour
  3. Mashed the potatoes in a bowl
  4. Then add the other ingredients:
    • Cheese
    • Small Bacon Pieces
    • 1/3 of Milk
    • Butter
    • Sliced Green Onions
  5. Combined them together
  6. Put it in oven Bake 400 and for 10 minutes
